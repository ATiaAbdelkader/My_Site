GET TO KNOW ME

About Me

I'm Abdelkader Atia, an agronomy professional with 12+ years of experience in agricultural engineering, professional training, and project supervision, I am currently pursuing a PhD in Agronomy (Animal Production), building on a Master's in Plant Production and an Engineer's degree in Arid Region Animal Breeding.

My work spans agricultural engineering, pedagogical design, and digital innovation. I serve as a Specialized Teacher at Hassani Abdelkrim INSFP in EL-Oued, where I design vocational training programs, supervise agriculture operations, and mentor students in modern farming techniques. I am also the author of the book "On the Edge" (ISBN 9947-26-849-1).
